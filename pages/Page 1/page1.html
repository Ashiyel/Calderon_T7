Welcome to the World of One Piece

One Piece is a famous anime and manga created by Eiichiro Oda. It follows Monkey D. Luffy, a pirate who gained rubber powers after eating a Devil Fruit.

Main Goal

Luffy wants to become the King of the Pirates by finding the legendary treasure, the One Piece, left by Gol D. Roger.
